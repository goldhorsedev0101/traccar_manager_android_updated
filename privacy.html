Privacy Policy - GpsLink

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect latitude, longitude, speed, and direction data from linked GPS devices to provide the tracking service.
  • Mobile Device Data: If you use the app, we collect unique device identifiers to send push notifications.
  • Account Data: Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.

2. Background Location Usage

Critical Statement: "GpsLink collects location data to enable real-time tracking and route history, even when the app is closed or not in use. This data is necessary for features such as geofence alerts and vehicle security."

3. How We Share Information

  • We do not sell data: GpsLink does not sell or rent your location data to third parties for advertising purposes.
  • Service Providers: We share strictly necessary data with infrastructure providers (such as IONOS for hosting and Google Maps for visualization).

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Route data is stored for a period of 90 days.
  • Users can request the complete deletion of their account and data by sending an email to info@gpslink.com.

5. Security

We implement SSL (HTTPS) encryption for all communications between the tracker, the server, and the mobile application to prevent interception.

6. International Data Transfer

By using our service, you agree that your data may be processed on servers located in the United States, ensuring adequate levels of protection.
